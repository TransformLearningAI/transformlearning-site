## RECOMMENDED APPROACH

**For Presidents/Provosts:** Use Letter 1. Lead with the board conversation. They need evidence to show someone.

**For Deans/VPs:** Use Letter 2. Lead with the Tuesday afternoon problem. They need a tool, not a strategy.

**For Department Chairs:** Use Letter 3. Lead with faculty workflow. They need to know it's not more work.

**For Admissions/Enrollment:** Use Letter 4. Lead with the retention revenue math. They think in terms of yield and melt.

**Timing:** Send to Presidents first (they set institutional priorities). Follow up with Deans/VPs one week later. Then Chairs. The President email creates top-down awareness; the Chair email creates bottom-up demand.

**Follow-up:** If no response in 7 days, send a one-line follow-up: "Just checking if you had a chance to see this — happy to do a quick 15-minute walkthrough whenever works. No commitment, just a demo."
